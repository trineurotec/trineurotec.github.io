Disorders

Special research for neurodevelopmental disorders

Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) is a neurodevelopmental disorder which has three core symptoms; inattention, hyperactivity and impulsivity.

Our Service

Our company develop many neurodevelopmental disease models, do drug efficacy test by drug screening and consult about experiments and drugs.

Research

Specific research focusing on ADHD

Because of many experiences about conducting the National Grants related to neurodevelopmental disorders, our researches specifically focus on neurodevelopmental disorder. Recently, we got S grade from the Research Foundation in South Korea. Based on pharmacology, we develop effective and personalized drug for patients to reduce side effects of drug.

OUR FOCUS

Through developing the drug which has various discriminative choices, we modulate multiple targets flexibly and minimize adverse effects to improve efficacy, guarantee stability and expand usage on other various diseases
